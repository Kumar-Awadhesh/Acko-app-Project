What is Bike Insurance?

Bike insurance or two-wheeler insurance is an insurance policy that covers you against damages to your two-wheeler due to accidents, natural/man-made


disasters, fire, and theft. The motorcycle insurance policy also provides law-mandated coverage against third-party liabilities such as damage to another person’s


vehicle/property or injuries/death of a third party caused due to the insured two-wheeler. You can buy insurance for two-wheeler to get coverage for motorcycles,


scooters, mopeds, etc.
